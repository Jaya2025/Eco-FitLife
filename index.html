WIIMB
WHAT IS IN MY BRAIN?

Through daily health journaling, users can safely record their moods, triggers and experiences. WIIMB’s AI reads these entries, detects emotional patterns and builds a timeline of mental health events — turning invisible struggles into visible insights. Quick Chat, an AI companion, offers instant emotional support during moments of stress or anxiety, using past context to respond in an empathetic and personalised way. Community spaces allow users to connect anonymously with others facing similar challenges, creating a secure and judgement-free environment for sharing and learning.
For doctors and caregivers, WIIMB generates weekly progress reports and offers an AI-driven report analyzer that highlights mood trends, trigger points and behavioural shifts. This transforms fragmented information into actionable insights, helping professionals provide faster, more precise, and compassionate care.
By blending natural language processing, emotion detection and secure cloud technology, WIIMB turns passive data into active support. It is not just an app — it is a living, learning system that grows with each user’s journey, making mental health management more transparent, empowering and continuous.
With WIIMB, mental health care moves from being episodic and invisible to being ongoing, intelligent and empathetic — putting technology and humanity on the same side of your healing journey.
